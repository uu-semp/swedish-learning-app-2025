Swedish Language Learning App - Team 08

Say what?

A Swedish-language learning game,
match the images to audio

This game uses persistent storage to save your progress, guesses and selections.
By playing or pressing proceed, you agree to the use of persistent storage.

Gameplay

The following game challenges you to identify what image corresponds to the audio that you can play. There is a settings button in the top right corner that you can use to control the volume and enable/disable sound effects. Once you press start, you get to choose the categories that you want to be tested on. These will then be the pool that the game uses to draw different words from.

When you get into the game you will be able to press a play audio button to hear the word you are supposed to match to the images. To progress through the game, you select an image and if you are sure it corresponds to the audio, you press the confirm button. If you are wrong, you will get the opportunity to try again: press the "try again" button. If you are right, you get a new word to guess and this continues for ten words. After that, you proceed to the end screen and can restart the game.
