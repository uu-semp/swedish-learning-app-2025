Custom Vocabulary Manager

User-Managed Vocabulary Extension Guide

Required Fields:

English: The English translation of the Swedish word.

Swedish: The Swedish word you want to learn.

Category: Choose one or more categories that this word belongs to (e.g., colors, food, furniture). You can select multiple categories from the dropdown.

Optional Fields:

Swedish Plural: The plural form of the Swedish word (e.g., "äpplen" for "äpple").

Article: The Swedish article - either "en" or "ett". Click the same option again to deselect.

Literal: A literal translation or additional meaning of the word.

Image: Enter a URL or click "Upload Image" to select a file from your computer (max 2MB).

Image Copyright Info: Copyright information.

Audio: Enter a URL or click "Upload Audio" to select a file from your computer (max 5MB).

Tips:

  • Words are automatically checked for duplicates based on the English translation
  • All your custom words are saved locally and will be available next time you visit
  • You can delete individual words or clear all words at once
