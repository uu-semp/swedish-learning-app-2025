Educational Information

This Basic Swedish Language Game is made to help exchange students at Uppsala University learn Swedish in a fun way.

It lets players practice basic vocabulary and grammar through interactive activities and playful exercises.

The game mainly focus on clothing, so players can practice common Swedish clothing terms and the correct use of articles (en/ett).

Mostly of the words come from the Basic Swedish 1 course.

The game includes multiple levels with increasing difficulty, players can follow the instructions and complete the exercises step by step.

Players can receive immediate feedback on their answers and can track their progress as playing.

After completing the game, users can see the number of mistakes they made, which helps them know what to practice more.

Have fun and learn Swedish at the same time!

Created as part of the "Software Engineering and Project Management" course 2025
